Why Zeus

Zeus is a free online project management tool

Zeus is aimed at small to medium software businesses. It will provide you with straight forward project metrics so you can maximise your success. Zeus will help whether you work in agile ways or not, we think agile is a great way to work but we recognise it isn't suited to everyone.
